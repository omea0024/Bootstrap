Ways to reduce waste

with food

From saving food scraps, composting the rest or shopping locally keeps your carbon footprint low

at school or work

Bringing lunch from home or planning ahead with resuable options so less disposable resources are used

in your wardrobe

Thrifting gently used items and finding second hand alternatives are great options. Learning to sew and patch work also will help extend the life of your garments

with cleaning

Biodegradable is key to prevent alga blooms and microplastics from entering the water systems.From soap nuts to wool dryer balls, lots of options

at social events

Give experienes and less things, plan ahead and cook instead of last minute plastic vegetable trays.

with transportation

Bike, walk, car share and bus more often and use paperless alternatives to payment options
